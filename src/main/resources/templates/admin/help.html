Admin Help & FAQ

Select 'Users' from the side navigation or on the dashboard and search for the customer you want to edit. Then, click the 'edit' link on the right to begin editing.

You can edit the features your customer has displayed on their dashboard, and you are able to clear customer data for a particular metric, or delete their account.

Please contact the technical team if there are any further changes that are required outwith the options provided.

Please fill out the form on the Report Issue page with details of the issue that the customer is experiencing. You will receive a response within 2 working days.
